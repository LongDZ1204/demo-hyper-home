More About Minh

Background, working style, and fit notes for clients who want to understand the artist before booking.

Background

Pham Minh Phuc, known as Minh, is the founder of Hyper Inkers, the best tattoo parlor in San Antonio TX. He began tattooing at the age of 16 and has spent more than 10 years developing his craft.

Born in 1999, Minh studied graphic design in university before relocating to the United States in 2019. After adapting to a new country and new working environment, he earned a position at a premier tattoo shop in San Antonio, Texas.

Craft Approach

Since then, Minh has focused on rigorous self-learning and mentorship from talented local artists, building a strong foundation in his craft. Working alongside other skilled artists continues to push his creative growth.

He is deeply committed to development as an artist, shown through consistent self-study, adaptability, and active participation in competitions.

Specialization

Minh specializes in Black and Grey Realism, a style for which he is widely recognized and often sought out as the best black and grey tattoo artist in San Antonio. He also works in Color Realism and holds a deep appreciation for Oriental-style tattoos.
