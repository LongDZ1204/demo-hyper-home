The Piercing Process in San Antonio

The piercing process at Hyper Inkers in San Antonio is quick, walk-in, and built around doing it safely. Getting pierced by a trained professional means the placement suits your anatomy, the needle is single-use, and the jewelry is body-safe to heal in, so you can relax and enjoy the moment instead of worrying about it.

Here is what a piercing visit looks like from start to finish.

  1. Walk in with your ID

    No appointment is needed. Bring a valid, original, government-issued photo ID, such as a state ID, government ID, or passport. For a minor, bring the minor's birth certificate and a parent who has their own photo ID.

    Your piercer marks the spot, has you check it in the mirror, and pierces by hand in a few seconds.

  5. Aftercare before you go

    You leave with clear aftercare guidance and time to ask questions, so you know how to keep it healing clean.

Piercing Aftercare in San Antonio

Piercing aftercare at Hyper Inkers is simple: keep the area clean, leave the jewelry in, and follow the steps your piercer gives you. Here are the basics for a clean heal, and how long it usually takes.

  • Clean the piercing with sterile saline once or twice a day.
  • Leave the jewelry in and avoid twisting, rotating, or touching it while it heals.
  • Keep it dry and protected, and stay out of pools and hot tubs while it settles.
  • Healing times vary by placement and by person. An earlobe usually settles in about 6 to 8 weeks, while cartilage and many body piercings can take 6 months to a year.
  • Some swelling and tenderness is normal at first. If you have concerns, ask your piercer or a medical professional.
  • Follow your piercer's instructions, and read the full guide for your placement.
