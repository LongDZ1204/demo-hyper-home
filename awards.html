Hyper Inkers Awards40+ International Tattoo Competition Wins

Hyper Inkers is an award-winning tattoo studio in San Antonio, Texas. Since 2023, our artists, led by founder Minh Pham, have won more than 40 competition trophies, including 10 Best of Show titles, at over 20 tattoo conventions across 8 countries. This page records every Hyper Inkers award by year, from the 2026 New York Empire State Best of Show to wins at the San Antonio Tattoo Arts Festival, Golden State Expo, and Sinners Tattoo Expo, with the artists, the pieces, and the competitions behind each trophy.

Best of Show

New York Empire State Tattoo Expo 2026 · New York City, NY

Minh Pham won Best of Show at the New York Empire State Tattoo Expo 2026, the highest honor of one of the most prestigious conventions in the United States.
