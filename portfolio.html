Not sure which one fits?

A quick guide for choosing between new artwork, body jewelry, and fading old ink before your next step.

It depends on what you want. Choose tattoo for new custom artwork or a cover-up, piercing for ear, nose, septum, belly, or other body piercings, and laser removal to fade or erase an existing tattoo. If you're between options, our team can point you the right way in a quick consultation.

Yes. Hyper Inkers offers tattoo, piercing, and laser removal under one roof in San Antonio, so many clients fade an old tattoo with us and then book new artwork once the area is ready. Cover-up clients usually run a few laser fading sessions first, then book the new tattoo with the artist whose style fits the design.
