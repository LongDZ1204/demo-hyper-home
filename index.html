  1. Every tattoo at Hyper Inkers starts with your idea and is designed custom before any ink touches skin. We listen first and design second, and nothing is inked until you approve the sketch.   4. Hyper Inkers is owned and operated by tattoo artists, not a corporate chain. The owners answer for the quality of every piece, and that accountability is why no two tattoos here come off an assembly line.
