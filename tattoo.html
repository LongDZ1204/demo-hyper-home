Tattoo Aftercare in San Antonio

Caring for a new tattoo means keeping it clean, moisturized, and protected while it heals over the first two to four weeks. A new tattoo is an open wound, so proper aftercare is what prevents infection and preserves the art.

Wash it gently with antibacterial soap and warm water, then apply a thin layer of the ointment or lotion your artist recommends, and wear loose clothing. Keep the initial bandage on as instructed, and once it comes off, clean it and apply ointment. For the first two weeks, moisturize regularly and keep it out of the sun. Long term, keep the skin hydrated and use sunscreen on your tattoo.

Avoid swimming, direct sunlight, tight clothes, and picking scabs while it heals.

Always follow your artist's specific advice.
